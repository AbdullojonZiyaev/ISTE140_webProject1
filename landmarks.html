Famous Landmarks in Dubai

Dubai is known for its stunning architecture and iconic landmarks. Some of the must-visit places include:

  • Burj Khalifa: The tallest building in the world, offering breathtaking views of the city.
  • Burj Al Arab: A luxury hotel famous for its distinctive sail-shaped silhouette.
  • Dubai Mall: The world's largest shopping mall, featuring an indoor ice rink and an aquarium.
  • Dubai Marina: A stunning waterfront area filled with restaurants, shops, and skyscrapers.
